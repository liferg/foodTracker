foodTracker


Purpose

Tracking calories and macros can be a great tool for anyone looking to improve their health or fitness in some way, as it creates awareness and accountability around food. It can allow you to see whether or not you are eating enough to properly fuel your body, understand which macro and micronutrients are lacking in your diet, and it can be used as a tool to help with muscle gain and fat loss.

While tracking food can be a great tool with many benefits, it can easily become an obsessive or unhealthy habit. Constantly seeing how many calories you've eaten throughout the day can lead people to make decisions around food according to those numbers instead of listening to their body. It can cause feelings shame when they eat more calories than they deem appropriate, and are discouragement when they don't hit their goal macros to a tee.

However, there is a solution to this problem: foodTracker


How it works

  1. Set the times and days you will to allow yourself to view your calories and macros
  2. Go about your day as normal, tracking your food as you go -- without seeing your calories or macros!
  3. View your calories and macros once at your set times
  4. Assess and make any improvements to your diet accordingly
  5. Reach your health and fitness goals without sacrificing your mental health!

Who it's for

foodTracker is perfect for you if...

  • You're actively recovering from an eating disorder and are finding your way back to properly fueling your body
  • You have a history of unhealthy relationships with food but still want to track to help you gain muscle, lose fat, or make healthier choices for your body
  • You simply want to become more aware of what you are feeding your body without becoming food-obsessed and risking your mental health
